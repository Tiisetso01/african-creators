# Field Research — Finance Contact (Tuesday Session)

## Context

A contact who works in finance uses Excel to manually reconcile data across different systems — downloading spreadsheets from multiple sources and comparing them by hand. Tuesday is an opportunity to observe her actual workflow firsthand.

The specific system or company does not matter. The "download from different systems, compare manually in Excel" pattern is extremely common across finance, accounting, operations, and payroll. The pain is the same regardless of industry.

---

## What to Observe on Tuesday

- How many different systems is she downloading from?
- What is she actually checking — does X in system A equal X in system B?
- What happens when something doesn't match — how does she investigate?
- How long does the whole process take?
- How often does she do it — daily, weekly, monthly?
- What does she do with the result — does she report it to someone, action something, file it?

---

## Key Question to Ask Her

> "If you could skip the downloading and comparing part — and just see the mismatches immediately — what would you do with that extra time?"

This question separates the pain from the habit. Some people will say "it would save me 3 hours a week." That's a product.

---

## What to Write Down Afterwards

- The exact names of the systems she downloads from
- What the columns look like in each sheet
- What "matching" means to her — same amount? Same reference number? Same date?
- How she handles exceptions — what does she do when something doesn't match?
- Whether anyone else is involved in the process or reviews her work

---

## Why This Matters

This is primary research. Three real conversations with people doing the actual work will tell you more than any document. If she mentions the same pain unprompted that the accounting firms mentioned — manual comparison, missed mismatches, time lost — that is validation.

It may also reveal a standalone product opportunity separate from the accounting platform — a reconciliation tool that connects multiple systems and surfaces mismatches automatically, without the user ever opening Excel.
